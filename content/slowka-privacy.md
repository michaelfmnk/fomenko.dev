<h1>Słówka Privacy Policy</h1>
<p class="slowka-note">Last updated: April 25, 2026</p>

<h2>Data collection</h2>
<p>The developer does not collect, sell, or track personal data through Słówka. The dictionary database is bundled with the app and runs locally on your device.</p>

<h2>Local app data</h2>
<p>The app may store your recent lookups, saved words, setup choices, and settings on your device. This data is used only to provide app features and is not sent to the developer.</p>

<h2>Purchases</h2>
<p>In-app purchases and purchase restoration are handled by Apple. Słówka receives entitlement status from Apple so it can unlock dictionary access, but it does not receive your payment card details.</p>

<h2>Pronunciation audio and translation features</h2>
<p>Some entries may include remote pronunciation audio links from dictionary sources. Playing those files may contact the host of that audio file. If you enable Apple Translate, the word being translated may be sent to Apple unless the required language packs are downloaded for on-device translation. Translation behavior is handled by Apple's system translation features.</p>
