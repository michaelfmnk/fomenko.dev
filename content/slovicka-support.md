<h1>Slovíčka Support</h1>
<p class="slowka-lead">Help for Slovíčka, the offline Czech dictionary for iPhone and iPad.</p>

<h2>Contact</h2>
<p>For support, bug reports, purchase questions, or feedback, email <a href="mailto:michael@fomenko.dev">michael@fomenko.dev</a>.</p>

<h2>Common issues</h2>
<h3>Dictionary setup takes time</h3>
<p>On first launch, Slovíčka unpacks the bundled dictionary database to your device. Keep the app open until setup finishes.</p>

<h3>Pronunciation audio is unavailable</h3>
<p>Some dictionary entries include remote audio and some do not. When no audio file is available, Slovíčka uses the system speech voice as a fallback.</p>

<h3>Restore a purchase</h3>
<p>If you already bought full dictionary access, open Settings in the app and use Restore Purchase.</p>

<h2>Privacy</h2>
<p>Slovíčka is designed to keep dictionary use local to your device. Read the <a href="/slovicka/privacy/">privacy policy</a> for details.</p>
